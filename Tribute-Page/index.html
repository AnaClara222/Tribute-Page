One Direction

One Direction was a British-Irish boy band consisting of members: Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and previously, Zayn Malik. Formed in 2010, the band quickly rose to fame through their performances on the British television show The X Factor. They became one of the best-selling boy bands in history, with numerous hit singles and albums.
